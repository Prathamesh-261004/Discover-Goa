Must-Visit Places in Goa

Calangute Beach

Known as the 'Queen of Beaches', Calangute is the most popular beach in North Goa. With a lively atmosphere, water sports, beach shacks, and vibrant nightlife, it's perfect for party lovers and families alike.

Fort Aguada

This 17th-century Portuguese fort is a must-see heritage site in Goa. Overlooking the Arabian Sea, it offers panoramic views and a historic lighthouse. Fort Aguada is a symbol of Goa‚Äôs colonial past and coastal defense.

Dudhsagar Falls

A breathtaking four-tiered waterfall nestled in the Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary. Dudhsagar Falls is one of India‚Äôs tallest waterfalls and offers scenic hiking and nature experiences.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this 16th-century basilica holds the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier. Admire Baroque architecture, beautiful interiors, and the spiritual aura that attracts pilgrims worldwide.

Palolem Beach

A crescent-shaped beach in South Goa, Palolem is famous for its calm waters, scenic views, yoga retreats, and friendly beach shacks. Ideal for relaxation and experiencing Goa's laid-back vibe.

Anjuna Beach

Known for its trance parties and the famous Wednesday flea market, Anjuna Beach offers rocky cliffs, palm-lined shores, and a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure for travelers.
