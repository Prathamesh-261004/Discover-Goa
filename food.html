Authentic Goan Delicacies Served Dark & Delicious

Chicken Xacuti

A rich and aromatic Goan curry made with roasted spices and coconut, slow-cooked to perfection. A beloved festive dish often served with sannas (steamed rice cakes) or plain rice.

Goan Prawn Curry

This tangy and spicy prawn curry features fresh prawns simmered in a coconut milk base enriched with kokum and red chilies, capturing the coastal flavors perfectly.

Sorpotel

A spicy, tangy pork stew marinated with vinegar and spices. Traditionally slow-cooked to develop deep flavors, this dish is a staple at Goan Catholic celebrations.

Feni

Goa's signature spirit distilled from cashew fruit or coconut sap. Enjoyed responsibly, it holds cultural significance and a distinct local flavor.

Prawn Balchao

A fiery prawn pickle cooked with vinegar, red chilies, and spices. A spicy accompaniment that packs a punch and adds zest to everyday meals.
